About Me.

Hi! I’m Poonam Jangid, a passionate Graphic Designer and UI/UX Designer based in India. I bring ideas to life through thoughtful design, combining creativity with functionality to craft visuals and experiences that leave an impact.
With a strong foundation in Computer Science and a creative mindset, I bridge the gap between technology and design. My work ranges from eye-catching posters and flyers to intuitive mobile and web interfaces. I believe design is not just about making things look good—it's about solving problems and creating meaningful experiences.
I’m currently exploring design challenges, experimenting with layouts, typography, and interactions, and always seeking new ways to grow as a visual storyteller. Whether it's building brands, designing user flows, or creating pixel-perfect UI screens—I love what I do.
Let’s create something amazing together!
